FAQ


What are the 5 pillars of NJHS? NJHS is built upon the 5 pillars of character, scholarship, leadership, service, and citizenship- both at school and in our community.
How is NJHS related to Rancho ASB? NJHS is an organization completely separate from Rancho ASB. However, we work closely with ASB to plan on-campus events in order to prevent conflicting schedules.
Who can apply to Rancho NJHS? When can I apply? 8th graders at Rancho San Joaquin Middle School are eligible to apply to Rancho NJHS. Applications usually open around late September or October.
Is there a GPA requirement to apply to Rancho NJHS? Rancho NJHS applicants must have an overall 7th grade GPA of at least 3.75 (no more than 1 B grade per trimester).
